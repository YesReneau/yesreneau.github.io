My Tutoring Services

My tutoring covers everything from the specifics of U.S. college essay writing (e.g. how to structure the essay, how to make a sentence 'pop', etc.) to the generalities of the U.S. application process (e.g. what to include in your application, how to know what your application is saying about you, etc.).

My rates are the following (and subject to change):

  • Face-to-face online tutoring: $60/hour (I charge by the half hour)
  • Long essay (501+ words): $45/essay revision
  • Medium essay (251-500 words): $22.50/essay revision ($45 minimum per student)
  • Short essay (0-250 words): $10/essay revision ($30 minimum per student)

    • For the face-to-face online tutoring, I use Google Hangouts. For the essay revisions, you would send me your essays, I would read them, and then I would send them back with revisions via email.

    I prefer that all of the payments are processed through Venmo (but if that doesn't work for you, we can discuss other payment options). For face-to-face tutoring, I ask for my students to send me payment at least 24 hours before the Google Hangout session. For essay revisions, I ask my students to send payment at the time that they send me their essay (give or take a few minutes).

    If all of this sounds good, email me at yesreneau@gmail.com and we can discuss it further. Additionally, if you have any questions, don't hesitate to ask. Talk to you soon!
